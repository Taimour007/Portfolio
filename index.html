About Me

The Engineering Philosophy

I am a Mechanical Design Engineer specializing in complex mechatronic systems, iterative rapid prototyping, and high-performance hardware architecture. I do not just build 3D models. I engineer physical realities.

From leading the aerodynamic design of an award-winning electric vehicle to reverse engineering industrial agricultural systems, my focus is strictly on Design for Manufacturability (DFM) and computational validation.

Whether it needs to be simulated in ANSYS, iterated in PETG, or modeled with micro-millimeter precision to bridge the gap between theoretical physics and functional hardware, I am the engineer who executes it.
